Send messages without hassle!

Tired of saving contacts just to send a quick message? Zapify solves that.

- Avoid cluttering your contact list: open chats on WhatsApp, Telegram, and Signal without saving the number
- Easily view and reuse recent numbers
- Customize which messaging apps you want to see
- Set a default country code
- Supports light and dark mode

Simple, fast, and straightforward.
